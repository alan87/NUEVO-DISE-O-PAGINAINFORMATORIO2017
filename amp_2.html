¿What is Amo los perros?

It is a non-profit organization, performs assistance work for both stray animals and low-income owners, in addition to the weekly organization of puppy adoption fairs in the Plaza San Martín and castration days at low cost. In the outstanding work of the volunteers, the rescue of animals that once recovered are located in new responsible homes is also outstanding. I Love the Dogs also collaborates with the municipality of Sáenz Peña in the implementation of free castrations in the most needy neighborhoods, decision of the Intendancy that means a valuable contribution to solve the serious health problems that generate animals that roam the neighborhoods in terrible conditions.

Experiences 2017-Amo los perros

We have the presence of the members of the organization I Love the Dogs, in which they told us what their needs are for which they need a software, which will also be presented at the end of the course of the Informatory as Final Work. In order to understand a little more, we went to the place where the castration campaign was carried out, in which we saw that in order to obtain data from those responsible for the animals and the animals themselves, they were made on paper sheets, as well as also the accounting of the money of castrations at low cost. We also obtained information from their social networks like Facebook, which provided us with a lot of useful information for the realization of this software. Once we obtained a part of the software, we went back to the castration campaign to carry out tests of its operation, as well as to obtain the opinion of the users and of the same organization; the result was good, they were able to register the pets and their owners, also check that the system works correctly. Once more information was obtained and the functioning of the system was checked, it was continued to improve it and add more functions that the organization needs. The team was divided into two parts: Backend and Frontend; we worked with systems such as Django and Materialize, before implementing the system we focused on making it a simple system so that users do not have problems when using it as well as understand what they should do when filling out forms to obtain shifts of castration and others.
